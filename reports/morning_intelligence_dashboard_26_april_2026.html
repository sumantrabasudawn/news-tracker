Executive Read

The current configuration is not a generic risk-off market. It is a more selective repricing regime: global equities remain supported, but India is being re-scored through oil sensitivity, foreign outflows, valuation risk and trade-policy optionality.

FTA Intelligence

India’s trade outreach is no longer a purely export-market exercise. It is a hedge against tariff volatility, supply-chain fragmentation and geopolitical concentration.

De-dollarisation

De-dollarisation is occurring at the margin, not at the core. The dollar’s reserve share has drifted lower, but FX system plumbing remains USD-led.
